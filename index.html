Lightchain is a Decentralized Autonomous Banking System

The Lighchain network is a decentralized, off-chain, peer-to-peer network. It brings together individual servers that automatically form voting pools. Once connected, the servers vote when funds are moved in such a way that scheme security is distributed. Anyone can host a server and connect to other servers that are willing to host funds. The solution to the potential of collusion, or the introduction of malware such as badBIOS, is to use proof of guarantee through Bitcoin’s scripting capabilities.

Lighchain Decentralized Banking

Secure

Bitcoin users that have to rely on a third party to handle their funds have been victims of heists, thefts, hacks, and scams. We are building an autonomous escrow system where there are no longer single points of failure.

Decentralized

Lighchain operates on the Blockchain - a cryptographically secured public ledger distributed amongst all of its millions of users around the world. The Lighchain network uses multisignature transactions to ensure consensus amongst its users.

Autonomous

Lighchain allows anyone to become part of its network by setting up an escrow server. Members of the network can earn fees by playing a part in the autonomous network.
